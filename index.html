Health Effects

  • PM2.5 & PM10: Tiny particles that can penetrate deep into lungs and bloodstream. Can cause respiratory and cardiovascular issues.
  • Ozone (O₃): Can trigger asthma, reduce lung function and cause lung diseases. Ground-level ozone is harmful, unlike stratospheric ozone.
  • Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂): Can inflame airways and increase susceptibility to respiratory infections and allergens.

Protective Measures

  • When AQI is poor, limit outdoor activities, especially for sensitive groups.
  • Keep windows closed during high pollution events.
  • Consider using air purifiers indoors.
  • Wear appropriate masks (N95/KN95) when pollution levels are high.
